Jaime.

Fashion + Branding - Spring 2018

Jaime, pronounced /Jame/ , is a practice in fashion design, branding, and trend observing. The premise of the project was to assume the role of a brand contracting a t-shirt and denim line. I wanted to not only found a fictional brand, but also give it its distinctive and demanding brand identity
