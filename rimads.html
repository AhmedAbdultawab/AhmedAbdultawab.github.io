Project Stork

Destigmatizing Medicine

how do you change the way people think about process of procuring medicine and alleviate some of the negative connotations that inherently stick to any transaction in this medium?

That was the main design challenge that inspired my approach to Stork , Rimads' medicine and health items delivery app.

Concepts

the first stage was redesigning the flow by addressing the problem through its roots

Take a Deep Breath.

I've redisgned the app to have as much breathing room as possible. This meant using a combination of overflowing whitespace and subtle highlights, while keeping buttons and interactable objects colorful and contrasty.

Iconography

a big challenge was changing the way shopping for health products is viewed through playful and colorful iconography
