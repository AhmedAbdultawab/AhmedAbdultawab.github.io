Project Stork

What
is it?

Stork is a pharmaceutical app that had a simple mission: make it easier to connect users to health products.

This was achieved thorugh two main features; a subscription service thorugh which you can buy and get medicine delivered, and a consultation service that connects you to health experts thorugh the app.

What
Did I Do?

As I served as the sole designer and iOS developer in this ambitious tech startup, I've had the chance to re-conceptualize the app's approach to usability, design and implement a newly friendly brand identity, and finally code up the app's functionality with Swift.

Skills Used

• Web Design and Development
• User experience Design for Stork
• iOS Development for the App
• Graphic Design for the Iconography
• Brand Identity Development

Concepts

the first stage was redesigning the flow to focus on making it easy to use, and highly accessible to audience with no medical background

Take a Deep Breath.

I've redesigned the app to have as much breathing room as possible. This meant using a combination of overflowing whitespace and subtle highlights, while keeping buttons and interactable objects colorful and contrasty.

Iconography

a big challenge was changing the way shopping for health products is viewed through playful and colorful iconography.

Iterative
Development

The project went through numerous iterations that involved shorter development cycles. I used sketch for prototyping, and Swift for front-end implementation
